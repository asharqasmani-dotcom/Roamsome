About ROAMSOME

Travel-inspired meal kits with a premium but simple point of view

ROAMSOME is a travel-inspired meal kit service bringing global cuisines to your home in a premium yet simple way.

Brand story

Built for people who love the feeling of discovering something new

ROAMSOME began with a simple idea: dinner at home can feel as exciting as travel when the ingredients, stories and flavours are chosen with care. We wanted to create a food subscription brand that feels polished and premium without becoming intimidating or overly complex.

Mission

Make premium global cooking accessible for everyday UK homes

Our mission is to help people cook with confidence, explore more cuisines and enjoy a flexible subscription that keeps pace with modern life. Every box is designed to reduce friction while increasing delight.

Experience

Carefully considered from menu design to doorstep delivery

We obsess over the full experience: the quality of produce, the pace of the recipes, the visual presentation of the menu and the ease of managing your subscription. The result is a weekly ritual that feels calm, premium and memorable.
